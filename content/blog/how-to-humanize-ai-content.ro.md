AI ka likha hua content ek hi wajah se ignore hota hai: woh AI jaisa lagta hai. Parhne wale seconds mein mehsoos kar lete hain, chahe woh bata na sakein ke masla kya hai. Achi khabar yeh hai ke yeh "AI wali awaaz" koi jaadu nahi aur na hi ittefaq. Yeh chand tay shuda aadaton se aati hai, aur har ek ko hataya ja sakta hai.

Is article mein aap dekhenge ke woh aadatein kya hain, Claude (ya kisi bhi AI) se un se aage ki tehreer kaise likhwayen, aur roz roz wohi hidayat dohrane ke bajaye is fix ko ek reusable skill se automatic kaise banayen.

## AI ki tehreer robotic kyun lagti hai

Models ko mehfooz, mutawazan aur organized rehne ki training di jaati hai. Accuracy ke liye mufeed, awaaz ke liye tabahkun. Nateeja chand aisi nishaniyan hain jo taqreeban har khaam AI draft mein nazar aati hain:

- **Ek jaisi raftaar.** Har jumla taqreeban ek hi lambai ka. Insani tehreer kahin tez chalti hai, kahin thehr jaati hai.
- **Ratte rataye phrases.** "Aaj ki tez raftaar duniya mein", "delve into", "game-changer", "unlock the power of". Koi insaan aise baat nahi karta.
- **Har baat mein ehtiyat.** "Yeh mumkina tor par behtari mein madad de sakta hai..." Jis ke paas raye ho woh seedha batata hai ke cheez karti kya hai.
- **Khali tamheed aur khali ikhtitam.** Ek paragraph jo sirf topic ka elaan karta hai, aur aakhir mein wohi baat dohra di jaati hai. Dono kuch nahi kehte.
- **Had se zyada structure.** Har jagah bullets aur headings, wahan bhi jahan behta hua paragraph baat behtar pohanchata.
- **Tafseel ka fuqdaan.** Abstract dawe ("efficiency behtar hoti hai") baghair kisi thos tafseel ke jo koi asli insaan sach mein mehsoos karta.

> Parhne wale AI content is liye reject nahi karte ke kisi detector ne pakar liya. Is liye karte hain ke woh kisi ka bhi nahi lagta.

## Ilaaj qismat nahi, hidayat hain

Zyada tar log isay dobara generate kar ke theek karne ki koshish karte hain: "isay zyada insani banao." Is se thora farq parta hai, ek baar. Asal ilaaj yeh hai ke AI ko bilkul bataya jaye ke kya badalna hai, kyunke upar wali nishaniyan makhsoos hain aur durust ho sakti hain:

1. **Usay ek awaaz dein.** "Professional tone" nahi. Batayen ke bol kaun raha hai: "ek founder jo doosre business owners ko likh raha hai, seedha, pur-aitmaad, baghair bharti ke."
2. **Apni tehreer ke samples dein.** Aapki asli tehreer ke do teen paragraphs kisi bhi sift se behtar hain. AI misalon se raftaar aur alfaaz ka intikhab kahin behtar pakarta hai.
3. **Pabandiyon ke naam lein.** Woh phrases aur ehtiyati alfaaz ginwa dein jo aap kabhi nahi chahte. Wazeh list kaam karti hai; "cliches se bacho" nahi karta.
4. **Tafseel maangein.** Har section mein ek thos tafseel maangein. Tehreer mein insani hone ki sab se mazboot nishani yehi hai.
5. **Buland awaaz mein parhne ka kahein.** Lafzan hidayat dein: "jo jumla tum kisi saathi se na kaho, usay dobara likho." Hairat angez had tak muassir filter hai.

Nayi chat mein yeh sab karein to acha draft milta hai. Magar masla yeh hai: kal aapko yeh sab dobara type karna parega.
