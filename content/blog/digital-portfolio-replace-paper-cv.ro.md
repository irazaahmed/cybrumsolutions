Aaj se das saal pehle job interview par jaane ka matlab tha — CV print karo, folder mein rakh lo, saath mein degree certificates, experience letters aur jo bhi mangein woh le jao. Ghar par copies rakhi hoti thi. Raat ko printer mein ink khatam ho jaati to subah ki takleef alag hoti.

Woh zamana ab wapas nahi aayega.

## Kya badla

Yeh tabdili is liye nahi aayi ke technology fashionable ho gayi. Yeh is liye aayi ke faisla karne wale log — employers, clients, idare, saath kaam karne wale — aap ko check karne ka tarika badal gaya.

Aap kamre mein dakhil hone se pehle hi, woh aap ko dhundh chuke hote hain. Naam likha, LinkedIn dekha, do minute click kiya. Jo mila ya nahi mila, us ne unki raay bana di — kuch bolne se pehle hi.

Printed CV us lamhe mein nahi ho sakta. Ek link ho sakta hai.

## Digital portfolio asal mein kya hota hai

LinkedIn profile nahi. Google Drive par upload ki hui PDF nahi. WhatsApp status par certificate ki photo nahi.

Digital portfolio internet par aapki apni jagah hoti hai — aapka naam, aapka kaam, aapki sanad, aapka contact — sab ek professional page par, jo kisi bhi screen par chand seconds mein khuljaye. Koi aapka link type kare ya QR code scan kare aur sab kuch wahan ho: aap kaun hain, kya kiya hai, kya kar sakte hain, kaise rabta karein.

Ek link. Sab kuch us mein.

## Iske baghair aap kya kho rahe hain

Aam zindagi mein kya hota hai jab aapki digital presence nahi hoti.

Ek company hire karna chahti hai. Do candidates barabar qualified hain. Ek link bhejta hai, doosra PDF attachment. Link wala zyada serious lagta hai. Attachment inbox mein ek baar khuli, shayad, aur phir dab gayi.

Ek client ko mashwarah chahiye. Logon se poochha, aapka naam aya. Google kiya. Kuch nahi mila. Agla naam liya aur aage badh gaye.

Kisi event mein mile, contact exchange hua, koi credentials follow up karna chahta hai. Aap ne WhatsApp par certificate ki photo bheji. Professional nahi lagi. Confidence inspire nahi hua.

In mein se har case mein, ek link nateeja badal sakta tha.

## Yeh sirf corporate professionals ke liye nahi

Sabse aam ghalat faihmi yeh hai ke digital portfolio sirf software developers ya designers ke liye hota hai. Yeh galat hai.

Agar aap **doctor** hain — aapki qualifications, specialization aur clinic ki details ek aise page par honi chahiye jo log dhundh sakein.

Agar aap **ustaad ya academic** hain — aapki education, publications aur tajurba ek click par hona chahiye.

Agar aap **business owner** hain — aapka background aur jo aap offer karte hain woh sirf WhatsApp business profile mein nahi rehna chahiye.

Agar aap **fresh graduate** hain — ek portfolio page aap ko ek inbox mein pade sau CVs se alag karta hai.

Aur agar aap **Aalim ya Islamic Scholar** hain — jinhon ne saalon mein ijazahs hasil ki hain, specialized Islamic training mukammal ki hai, scholarly kaam ka bada silsila hai — aapki sanad ek folder mein ya certificate ki phone photos ke tor par nahi rehni chahiye.

## Ulama aur Islamic Scholars ke liye khaas baat

Islamic scholars ke paas credentials hote hain jo waqai mein qabil e taarif hain: barson ki structured study, baray scholars se ijazah, Islamic knowledge ke specialized fields mein maharat, tarjuma ka kaam, advisory ki zimmadari. Yeh credentials un logon ke liye matter karte hain jinhe aapki rehnumaai chahiye.

Lekin aksar scholars ki koi digital presence nahi hoti. Unki qualifications kagazon mein hain, folder mein hain, ya unhi logon ki yaad mein jo inhe personally jaante hain. Us daaire ke bahar, woh invisible hain.

Ek professional portfolio page yeh sab badal deta hai. Aapki scholarly background ko usi sanjidagi ke saath pesh karta hai jis ki woh mustahiq hai, aur institutions, publications aur afraad ke liye aasaan banata hai ke woh aap ko dhundh sakein aur aapki sanad verify kar sakein.

## Shuru karne ke liye kya chahiye

Jitna aap sochte hain us se kahin kam.

Websites ya technology ke baare mein kuch jaanna zaroori nahi. Sirf yeh jaanna zaroori hai ke aap kaun hain aur aap ne kya kiya hai. Baaki sab hum karein ge: design, content organization, hosting, domain setup, aur yeh yaqeen dilana ke yeh laptop aur phone dono par properly professional dikhe.

Ghar bethe, kuch hi dinon mein, aapke paas ek aisa link ho sakta hai jo aapko theek se represent kare.
