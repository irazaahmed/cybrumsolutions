Har kuch mahine baad AI ki duniya mein koi naya lafz cha jaata hai. Aaj kal woh lafz hai "AI agents." Log isay har cheez ke liye use karte hain, ek saadi si chatbot se le kar poore self-running system tak, isi liye is lafz ka matlab taqreeban khatam ho gaya hai. To chaliye saaf saaf baat karte hain ke AI agent asal mein hota kya hai, aur is se bhi zyada zaroori, ek asli business mein iski jagah kahan banti hai.

## AI agent asal mein hota kya hai

Aam chatbot ek sawal ka jawab deti hai aur ruk jaati hai. AI agent is se alag hai. Aap usay ek goal dete hain, aur woh us goal ki taraf khud kaam karta hai: kya steps lene hain woh khud sochta hai, jo tools chahiye woh khud use karta hai, result check karta hai, aur agar kuch ghalat ho jaye to khud adjust kar leta hai.

Sab se aasan tareeqa isay samajhne ka:

> Chatbot baat karti hai. Agent kaam karta hai.

Ek agent aane wali email parh sakta hai, aapke database se zaroori record nikaal sakta hai, jawab draft kar sakta hai, tracking sheet update kar sakta hai, aur sirf woh chand cases flag kar sakta hai jinhe insaan ki zaroorat hai. Yeh poora silsila, jo bina kisi ke ek ek cheez par click kiye khud decide aur execute hota hai, yahi cheez usay ek "fancy autocomplete" se nikaal kar asal agent banati hai.

## AI agents kahan sach mein kaam aate hain

Yeh systems banane ke baad pattern bilkul saaf hai. Agents wahan apni jagah banate hain jahan kaam shakal mein to ek jaisa hota hai lekin tafseel mein har baar thora alag:

- **Inbox aur requests sambhalna.** Sorting, routing, jawab draft karna, aur woh cases aage bhejna jinhe sach mein kisi insaan ki zaroorat hai.
- **Data entry aur reconciliation.** Aise systems ke beech information move karna jo kabhi ek dusre se baat karne ke liye banaye hi nahi gaye thay, aur ghaltiyan masla banne se pehle pakar lena.
- **Research aur summary.** Bahut sare sources se maloomat ikatthi karna aur usay ek chhoti, decision ke liye tayyar brief mein badalna.
- **Multi-step kaam.** Naya customer onboard karna, order process karna, ya lead ko follow up karna, jahan har step pichle step par depend karta hai.

In sab mein kaam itna mukhtalif hai ke ek rigid script se nahi hota, lekin itna repeat hota hai ke kisi insaan ka poora din lene ke layak bhi nahi. Bilkul isi gap mein ek agent fit hota hai.

## Agents kahan kaam ke nahi

Apni hadood ke baare mein sach bolna hi woh cheez hai jo chalne wale system ko toot-ne wale demo se alag karti hai:

- **Bari aur high-stakes decisions ka aakhri faisla.** Agent faisla tayyar kar sakta hai. Lekin jab paisa, safety, ya bharosa daon par ho to call insaan hi ko leni chahiye.
- **Aisa kaam jiska "done" hi clear na ho.** Agar aap khud nahi bata sakte ke kamyabi kaisi dikhti hai, to agent bhi wahan nahi pohanch sakta.
- **Ek baar ka kaam.** Agar koi cheez sirf ek baar honi hai, to usay automate karne mein khud karne se zyada kharcha aata hai.

Maqsad kabhi insaan ko hatana nahi hota. Maqsad beech ka repeat hone wala hissa hatana hota hai, taake insaan apna waqt usi cheez par lagaye jis mein soch aur faisle ki zaroorat hai.

## Woh cheez jahan zyada tar log ghalti karte hain

Zyada tar AI projects is liye fail nahi hote ke model kaafi smart nahi tha. Woh is liye fail hote hain ke kisi ne asal workflow pehle samjha hi nahi. Agent utna hi acha hota hai jitna acha woh process hai jis ke andar woh baitha hai. Agar steps, data, aur "done" ki definition clear nahi, to koi bhi model usay nahi bacha sakta.

Isi liye hum hamesha workflow se shuru karte hain, model se nahi. Pehle samajhte hain ke waqt kis cheez mein lagta hai, kya toot-ta hai, aur kya repeat hota hai. Phir us ke ird gird system design karte hain. Intelligence to ab aasan hissa hai. Asal kaam us ke ird gird ki engineering hai, yahi woh jagah hai jahan projects jeetay ya haaray jaate hain.

## Khulasa
