Mukhtasir jawab pehle. Zapier shuru karne ke liye sab se aasan hai, Make kam paison mein zyada kaam deta hai, aur n8n teeno mein sab se powerful hai, khaas tor par jab AI shamil ho. Agar aapki automations saadi hain aur budget masla nahi, to Zapier theek hai. Agar aap barh rahe hain aur kharche par nazar hai, to Make. Aur agar aapko asal logic, AI steps aur poore control wali automation chahiye, to n8n.

Yeh to khulasa tha. Baqi article iski wajah hai, aur yeh teeno tools par asli businesses ke liye automation banane ke tajurbe par mabni hai.

## Yeh tools asal mein karte kya hain

Teeno ek hi masla hal karte hain: aapke business ki maujooda apps ko aapas mein jorna taake kaam un ke darmiyan khud ba khud chalta rahe. Naya order aaya to invoice ban gayi. Form submit hua to CRM mein entry aur WhatsApp message chala gaya. Rozana ki report khud tayyar ho gayi.

Farq is mein nahi ke yeh kin apps ko jorte hain. Mashhoor apps teeno ke paas hain. Farq is mein hai ke yeh workflows ke baare mein kaise sochte hain, paise kis cheez ke lete hain, aur deewar se takrane se pehle kitni door ja sakte hain.

## Zapier: aasan wala

Zapier sab se mashhoor naam hai, aur yeh shohrat usne do apps ko jorne ka sab se saada tareeqa ban kar kamai. Trigger chunein, action chunein, paanch minute mein kaam khatam.

- **Khoobiyan:** sab se bari app directory, sab se halka learning curve, aur aisi reliability jis par non-technical teams bharosa kar sakein. Agar business ka owner khud automation bana raha hai, to Zapier sab se mehfooz aghaz hai.
- **Khamiyan:** pricing. Zapier har task ke paise leta hai, aur workflow ka har step gina jaata hai. Jo workflow din mein sainkron baar chale woh tezi se mehnga ho jaata hai. Complex logic (branching, loops, data ki khaas handling) mumkin to hai lekin jald hi tool se larai mehsoos hone lagti hai.

> Zapier pehli automation ke liye behtareen hai. Pachaaswi ke liye shaz o nadir hi.

## Make: volume wala

Make (purana naam Integromat) darmiyan mein baithta hai. Iska visual canvas poora workflow jure hue modules ki shakal mein dikhata hai, jis se darmiyani complexity ki logic dekhna aur theek karna Zapier ki nisbat kahin aasan ho jaata hai.

- **Khoobiyan:** pricing. Make bhi per operation paise leta hai lekin iske plans kahin generous hain, is liye high-volume workflows aksar Zapier ke muqable mein bohat kam kharche mein chalte hain. Visual builder branching aur routing ko bhi zyada natural andaaz mein sambhalta hai.
- **Khamiyan:** learning curve asli hai, aur lambi chains par error handling uljh sakti hai. Yeh bhi ek band platform hai: aap Make ki hadood ke andar chalte hain, aur bhaari custom logic ko aakhir kaar jagah kam parne lagti hai.

## n8n: powerful wala

n8n woh tool hai jis ki taraf hum sab se zyada jaate hain, aur yehi hamare apne stack par hai. Iska code khula available hai, aap isay apne server par khud chala sakte hain (aapka data aapke server par rehta hai aur license ka kharcha zero tak aa sakta hai), aur yeh code ko majboori nahi balke feature samajhta hai: jahan kisi step ko asal logic chahiye, aap workflow ke andar hi code likh dete hain.

- **Khoobiyan:** gehrai. AI ke apne nodes aur agent patterns, LangChain integration, custom code steps, self-hosting, aur sainkron steps wale workflows jo samajh mein bhi aate hain aur aapke apne bhi rehte hain. AI wali automation ke liye, message parhna, faisla karna, model bulana, result par amal karna, is teeno mein koi iske qareeb nahi.
- **Khamiyan:** teeno mein sab se zyada technical yehi hai. Non-technical teams n8n cloud aaram se use kar sakti hain, lekin self-hosting aur advanced workflows builders ka maidan hai. Aur aam tor par hum yahin kaam aate hain.

> Saada kaam har tool mein fit hai. AI jaisa kaam n8n mein fit hai.

## Woh usool jo hum asal mein use karte hain

Jab koi business hum se poochta hai ke kaun sa tool chunein, to imandarana jawab teen sawalon ki tarteeb hai:

1. **Yeh din mein kitni baar chalega?** Kam volume Zapier ke haq mein hai. Zyada volume Make ya n8n ke haq mein, kyunke per-task pricing kamyabi ki saza deti hai.
2. **Kya kisi step ko soch kar faisla karna hai?** Agar kisi step ko kuch parh kar faisla karna hai (koi inquiry, invoice, ya lead), to workflow mein AI chahiye, aur yeh palra bhaari andaaz mein n8n ki taraf jhuka deta hai.
3. **Isay sambhalega kaun?** Agar owner khud sambhalega, to woh tool chunein jo woh sach mein chala sake. Behtareen tool bhi bekaar hai agar business mein koi usay chhoo na sake.

Ghaur karein ke is list mein kya nahi hai: tool ki shohrat. Pehle workflow, tool sab se aakhir mein.
