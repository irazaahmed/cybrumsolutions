Har imam is Jumeraat ki raat ko jaanta hai. Topic tay ho chuka hai. Congregation maloom hai. Khaali page apne aap kahin nahi jaane wala. To khutbah wesay hi banti hai jaise hamesha se banti aayi hai: haath se likha hua outline, opening line jo chaar baar dobara likhi jaati hai, aur doosra hissa jaldi mein nipta diya jaata hai kyunke waqt khatam ho jaata hai.

Yeh drafting ka kaam, structure, transitions, amaali baatein, aur closing, bilkul woh cheez hai jo AI aap se le sakta hai. Sirf ek cheez hai jise woh kabhi haath na lagaye: khud ayat aur Hadith.

## Woh asal khatra jis par koi baat nahi karta

Kisi bhi aam AI chatbot se sabr par khutbah likhwa kar dekhein, woh khushi khushi ek Quran ki ayat, ek Hadith, yahan tak ke exact number bhi bilkul confidence ke saath de dega. Kuch theek hoga. Kuch subtle taur par ghalat hoga: ghalat Surah, ek paraphrase jo seedha quote ban ke pesh ho jaye, ya woh Hadith jo us collection mein mojood hi nahi jiska naam liya gaya. Jab koi scholar yeh mimbar se sunata hai, to woh us congregation ko ghalat quote sunata hai jo usse mukammal etimaad rakhti hai.

Yeh koi choti si bug nahi jise bardasht kiya jaye. Deeni content ke liye, yeh woh akela failure hai jo ek dafa bhi hone ki ijazat nahi. Isi liye hum ne yeh assistant is rule ke gird banaya jo har doosre feature se pehle aata hai.
