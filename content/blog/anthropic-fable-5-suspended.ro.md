12 June 2026 ko US government ne ek export control directive jari kiya jis ne Anthropic ko apne do sabse naaye frontier AI models, **Fable 5** aur **Mythos 5**, ka access band karne par majboor kar diya. Yeh suspension raat bhar mein lagoo ho gaya aur duniya bhar mein karoron users ka access cut ho gaya.

Sarkari wajah: national security. Lekin jab Anthropic ne actually evidence dekha, to maamla bahut zyada routine nikla. Aur company ne publicly, record par, apna aiteraz darj karaya hai.

## US government ka asal concern kya tha

Government ne Fable 5 aur Mythos 5 mein ek potential jailbreaking vulnerability ko flag kiya. Jailbreak ek aisi technique hai jis se AI model ko uski apni safety guidelines bypass kar ke kuch aisa karne par majboor kiya ja sakta hai jo woh refuse karne ke liye design hua ho.

Anthropic ne evidence review karne ke baad paya ke yeh method **model se ek specific codebase parh kar software ki kharaabiyan dhoondhne ko kaha** mein. Security ki bhasha mein, yeh standard defensive security kaam hai. Yeh wahi kaam hai jo koi bhi developer ya security team routinely karti hai, aur iska offensive misuse se koi taluq nahi.

Anthropic ka moqaf: yeh capability pehle se doosre AI models se available hai, jis mein OpenAI ka GPT-5.5 bhi shamil hai. Fable 5 ko market se hatana capability ko nahi hatata. Sirf zyada samajhdari se bani option ko hata deta hai.

## Yeh suspension itna mushkil kyun hai defend karna

Fable 5 khaas tor par **cybersecurity safeguards** ke liye banaya gaya tha jo average se zyada mazboot the. Yeh safety ke lihaaz se, khaas tor par cybersecurity domain mein, sabse zyada socha samjha models mein se ek tha.

To cybersecurity ke mazbooth protections wala model, cybersecurity concern par suspend ho gaya, jabke kamzor safeguards wale models poori tarah available hain. Yeh logic kaam nahi karta, aur Anthropic ne seedha yahi kaha.

## Anthropic ne publicly kya kaha

Anthropic ka jawab teen nuqton par saaf aur specific tha.

**Pehla:** Ek narrow jailbreak finding ki wajah se poora model suspend nahi hona chahiye. Agar yeh standard hota, to kisi bhi provider ka koi bhi AI model survive nahi kar sakta. Perfect jailbreak resistance ab tak exist nahi karti, poori industry mein. Yeh sirf Anthropic ka dawa nahi hai; yeh technology ka aaj ka sachcha haal hai.

**Doosra:** Anthropic ka safety approach "defense in depth" hai, yani ek kami wali perfect wall ki jagah kai overlapping protection layers. Jab ek layer mein koi gap ho, baaki pakdi rehti hain. Ek narrow jailbreak finding poori architecture ko nahi girata.

**Teesra:** Jo specific capability flag ki gayi hai woh Fable 5 ke liye unique nahi hai. Yeh competing models se widely available hai. Suspension users se zyada protected version hata deta hai, jabke kam protected alternatives baqaur rahte hain.

## Anthropic se abhi bhi kya available hai

Doosre tamam Anthropic models online hain aur is directive se unaffected hain. Claude aur poori existing model lineup normally kaam karti rahi. Sirf Fable 5 aur Mythos 5 is suspension ke tahat hain.

Zyada tar business use cases ke liye, jis mein writing, customer support, data analysis, aur automation shamil hain, existing Claude models kaam cover karte hain bina kisi rukawat ke.

## AI use karne wale businesses ke liye kya matlab hai

Agar aapka kaam specifically Fable 5 ya Mythos 5 par depend karta hai, to aapko suspension ke dauran ek alternative model par move karna hoga. Zyada tar applications ke liye, yeh ek seedha swap hai jis ka minimal impact hai.

Zyada ahem sawaal woh precedent hai jo ab set ho gaya hai.

Yeh **pehli dafa hai jab US government ne kisi bade AI provider ko live frontier model ko deployment ke dauran pull karne par majboor kiya**. Ab har AI company, AI infrastructure par build karne wala har enterprise, aur har government regulator yeh dekh raha hai ke yeh kaise hal hota hai.

Jo sawaalaat yeh uthata hai woh technical nahi balke structural hain: Jailbreak kise kaha jaata hai yeh kaun decide karta hai? Model suspend karne se pehle evidence ka kaunsa standard chahiye? Kya process technically sound hai ya primarily political caution se driven hai? Aur agar ek frontier model raat bhar short notice par pull ho sakta hai, to businesses is risk ke around kaise plan karein?

## Yeh Anthropic se aage kyun matter karta hai

AI industry ne is qisam ke real-time government intervention ke bagair zyada tar kaam kiya hai. Regulatory frameworks model deployment speeds se bahut peechhe rahi hain. Yeh case woh gap ek move mein band karta hai aur sabse zyada disruptive tarike se karta hai: live users se active product raat bhar mein pull ho gaya.

Anthropic ka publicly disagree karna ahem hai. Woh chupchap comply nahi kar rahe. Woh faisle ki technical basis ko openly contest kar rahe hain, jis ka matlab hai yeh likely kisi process ke zariye hal hoga, na ke simple acceptance se. Yeh kaise khatam hoga, yeh future ke aise tamam cases ka reference point ban jaayega.

## Khulasa

Mazbooth safety protections ke sath bana ek frontier AI model ek aise concern par suspend ho gaya jo iske kam-protected competitors par bhi utna hi laagu hota hai. Government ne action liya; Anthropic ne publicly aiteraz kiya; precedent ab set ho gaya hai.

Businesses ke liye, fori amaliat impact limited hai. Jo bhi AI infrastructure par long term ke liye build kar raha hai, us ke liye government intervention risk aapki architecture mein kaise fit hota hai, yeh ab ek real sawaal hai, theoretical nahi.
