Aaj kal taqreeban har website par ek chatbot maujood hai. Inmein se zyada tar bekaar hain, aur customer do message ke andar hi yeh bhaanp leta hai. Yeh ghol mol jawab dete hain, ek lamha pehle jo kaha gaya woh bhool jaate hain, aur logon ko ghuma phira kar wahin le aate hain jab tak woh haar kar email na kar dein.

Masla technology ka nahi hai. Masla yeh hai ke yeh bots banaye kaise jaate hain. To yeh raha generic wrapper aur ek custom assistant ke darmiyan sacha farq, woh assistant jo sach mein apna kaam karta hai.

## Generic wrapper asal mein hota kya hai

Generic wrapper ek chat window hoti hai jo seedha ek language model se juri hoti hai, sath mein ek chhoti si hidayat ke "humari company ke baare mein sawalon ka jawab do." Bas itni hi baat. Aapke business se koi asli connection nahi hota.

Nateeja pehle se zahir hota hai:

- Jab jawab nahi pata hota to apni taraf se bana leta hai.
- Customer ka order, account, ya history yeh dekh hi nahi sakta.
- Topic badalte hi poori guftagu bhool jaata hai.
- Asal mein kuch kar nahi sakta, sirf baatein kar sakta hai.

Tees second ke liye modern lagta hai, aur phir pehle hi asli sawal par fail ho jaata hai.

## Custom assistant ke paas woh kya hai jo wrapper ke paas nahi

Asli assistant teen aisi cheezon ke ird gird bana hota hai jo wrapper ke paas nahi hotin:

**Knowledge.** Woh aapke asal content par bana hota hai: aapke products, policies, documentation, aur FAQs. Jab jawab deta hai to aapke business se deta hai, kisi andaze se nahi.

**Memory.** Woh guftagu yaad rakhta hai, aur jahan munasib ho wahan customer ko alag alag visits ke darmiyan bhi yaad rakhta hai. Ek hi maloomat teen baar nahi poochta.

**Tools.** Yeh sab se bari baat hai. Asli assistant action le sakta hai: order ka status check karna, appointment book karna, support ticket banana, ya poore context ke saath kisi insaan ke hawale karna.

> Wrapper aapke business ke baare mein baat karta hai. Assistant us ke andar kaam karta hai.

## Sacha test

Kisi bhi chatbot ko parakhne ka, apne wale samait, ek aasan tareeqa yeh hai. Us se aisi cheez poochein jis ke liye business ke baare mein asli, khaas knowledge chahiye, phir ek aisa follow up sawal poochein jo aapke pehle sawal par depend karta ho.

Wrapper foran bikhar jaata hai. Ya to jawab gharh leta hai ya silsila hi kho deta hai. Theek se bana hua assistant apni asal maloomat par qaayem rehta hai, context yaad rakhta hai, aur agar woh sach mein madad nahi kar sakta to saaf keh deta hai aur aapko kisi aise insaan tak pohanchata hai jo kar sakta hai. Yeh aakhri baat, apni hadood ka pata hona, yeh ek khoobi hai, kamzori nahi.

## Grounding model se zyada kyun maayne rakhti hai

Log is baat par bahut atak jaate hain ke chatbot kaun sa model use karta hai. Haqeeqat mein model bohat kam dafa faisla kun hota hai. Jo cheez tay karti hai ke assistant par bharosa hoga ya nahi woh yeh hai ke woh kitni achi tarah asal, taza knowledge par bana hai aur kitni safai se aapke systems se juta hai.

Ek achi tarah grounded assistant chahe ek maamuli model par ho, woh ek ungrounded assistant ko jo sab se advanced model par ho, har baar maat de ga, kyunke achi tarah grounded wala sach se jawab de raha hota hai, yaqeen se andaza nahi laga raha hota.

## Khulasa

Chatbot rakhne ka faida sirf tab hai jab woh sach mein madad kare. Is ka matlab hai asli knowledge, asli memory, aur asal action lene ki salahiyat, yeh sab aapke business jaise sach mein chalta hai us ke ird gird bana ho. Is se kam jo bhi hai woh ek wrapper hai, aur customer pehle hi seekh chuke hain ke aise bots ko nazar andaz kar dena hai.
