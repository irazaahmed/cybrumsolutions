Ek customer ko dry skin ke liye acha moisturizer chahiye. Ek saal pehle wo ye Google mein type karta, neeli links ka ek page scan karta, teen char kholta, aur compare karta. Aaj un mein se barhti hui tadaad ChatGPT kholti hai, sawal aam zubaan mein poochti hai, aur jawab mein ek ya do product names paati hai. Wo unhi mein se ek khareed leti hai. Na koi list dekhti hai, na aap ki website. Agar aap ka brand wo jawab nahi tha, to us customer ke liye aap ka wujood hi nahi tha.

Yahi khamosh tabdeeli is waqt search ke saath ho rahi hai. Ab kisi business ke online nazar aane ke teen alag tareeqe hain, aur zyadatar businesses apni saari mehnat sirf sab se purane wale par laga rahe hain.

## SEO: page par rank karna

SEO wo hai jo aap pehle se jaante hain. Ye aap ke pages ko Google par rank karwane ka kaam hai, keywords, backlinks, fast load time, aur achi tarah likhi descriptions ke zariye. Jab koi "best running shoes for flat feet" search kare, to SEO ye tay karti hai ke aap position ek par aate hain, position teen par, ya page do par.

SEO ab bhi zaruri hai. Ye wo bunyaad hai jis par baaqi sab cheezen khari hoti hain. Lekin page par rank karna ab nazar aane ke barabar nahi raha, kyunke us page ka top ab pehle jaisa nahi lagta.

## AEO: answer box mein aana

AEO ka matlab hai Answer Engine Optimization, aur ye us AI Overview ke baare mein hai jo ab Google results ke bilkul top par baithta hai. Aaj "best running shoes for flat feet" search karein to Google aksar khud hi ek box mein jawab de deta hai, kisi bhi ranked link se pehle. Wo product ka naam leta hai, wajah batata hai, aur zyadatar log wahi parh kar ruk jate hain.

Yahan takleef wali baat ye hai. Aap behtareen SEO ke saath number teen par rank kar sakte hain aur phir bhi invisible reh sakte hain, kyunke aap ke upar wala answer box customer ko wahi de chuka hai jo wo dhoondne aaya tha. AEO aap ke content ko is tarah structure karne ka kaam hai ke answer box aap se uthaye. Iska matlab hai saaf FAQ pages, imandar comparison guides, aur aisa content jo us khaas sawal ka seedha jawab de jo khareedaar type kar raha hai, sirf keywords se bhare pages nahi.

## GEO: recommendation ban jana

GEO ka matlab hai Generative Engine Optimization, aur ye teeno mein sab se naya hai. Ye ChatGPT jaise AI tools se aap ke business ya product ka naam le kar recommend karwane ke baare mein hai. Jab koi khareedaar AI assistant se poochta hai "dry skin ke liye acha moisturizer kya hai," to wo dus links nahi deta jin mein se chunna ho. Wo ek ya do recommendations deta hai. Agar un mein aap ka naam hai, to sale aap jeet jate hain. Agar nahi, to aap muqable mein hi nahi.

GEO, SEO se alag tareeqe se kaam karti hai. AI model aap ko rank nahi karta, wo usay recommend karta hai jo usne baar baar kahin aur recommend hote dekha hai. Iska matlab hai reviews, Reddit threads, niche publications, expert roundups, aur aap ki industry ke baare mein hone wali waseeh guftagu. AI usi ko recommend karta hai jis par internet pehle se bharosa karta hai.

## Number teen par rank karna kafi kyun nahi raha

Teeno ko mila kar dekhein to tabdeeli wazeh ho jati hai. Log ab scroll nahi karte. Wo poochte hain, aur jo pehla jawab milta hai usi par bharosa kar lete hain.

Sale tak pohanchne ka purana raasta aisa tha: ooncha rank karo, click hasil karo, umeed rakho ke convert ho jaye. Naya raasta alag hai: jawab ban jao, recommend ho jao, aur sale tab jeet lo jab koi competitor dikhaya bhi nahi gaya. Customer ka safar chhota ho gaya, aur jo businesses consideration tak bhi pohanchte hain un ki tadaad kam ho gayi. Ek ya do naam recommendation paate hain. Baaqi sab khareedaar ke faisla karne se pehle hi filter ho jate hain.

Ye koi door ki paishangoi nahi. Khareedaaron ka ek asal aur barhta hua hissa, Pakistan mein aur duniya bhar mein, pehle se roz isi tarah khareedari kar raha hai.

## Har ek par kaam kaise shuru karein

Achi baat ye hai ke ye teeno layers ek doosre ke upar jamti hain, ek doosre ki jagah nahi leteen. Aap inhein tarteeb se banate hain.

**SEO aap ki bunyaad rehti hai.** Apne product aur service pages optimize karte rahein, site fast rakhein, backlinks kamate rahein. In naye layers mein se koi bhi is base ke baghair kaam nahi karta.

**AEO asal sawalon ka jawab dene ke baare mein hai.** Apne content ko un theek sawalon ke gird structure karein jo aap ke customer poochte hain. Sahi FAQ pages banayein, imandar "best X for Y" guides likhein, aur comparison content banayein. Answer box wahi content uthata hai jo sawal ka seedha aur saaf jawab deta hai.

**GEO wahan mention hone ke baare mein hai jahan AI seekhta hai.** Apne business ki baat reviews, mutaliqa communities, industry publications, aur expert lists mein karwayein. AI assistants un brands ko recommend karte hain jo poore web par bharose ke saath nazar aate hain, sirf apni site par nahi.
