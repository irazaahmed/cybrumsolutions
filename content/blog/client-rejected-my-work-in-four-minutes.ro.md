Ek client ne mera kaam chaar minute mein reject kar diya.

Koi feedback nahi. Koi "let me think about it" nahi. Bas ek seedha "this isn't what I wanted," itni jaldi wapas aaya ke wo asal mein adha kaam bhi nahi parh sakta tha.

Mera pehla instinct defence tha. Aur meri wajhein thin. Achi wajhein. Brief vague tha, jo references unhone promise kiye the wo kabhi aaye hi nahi, aur scope kahin beech mein chupke se double ho chuka tha. Maine ek poora paragraph type kar dala, har point qatar mein, har fact mere haq mein.

Phir maine usse delete kar diya.

## Sahi hona kaam nahi hai

Yahan wo baat hai jo mujhe samajhni pari. Client aap ko sahi hone ke paise nahi deta. Wo aap ko is liye paise deta hai ke aap problem ko khatam kar dein.

Main wo behes jeet sakta tha aur phir bhi client ko kho sakta tha. Main brief, references, aur scope ke baare mein bilkul sahi ho sakta tha, aur theek us lamhe rishta khatam hote dekh sakta tha jab maine apni baat saabit ki. Sahi hona aur kaam ka hona ek cheez nahi hai, aur apne kaam ka defence karta paragraph sirf pehli cheez ke kaam aata.

To maine usse delete kiya aur ek hi line mein jawab diya.

"Got it. Tell me what's missing and I'll fix it today."

## Ek calm jawab ki maths

Revision mein chalees minute lage.

Rishta teen saal chala. Usi ek client ne us arse mein cheh referrals bheje. Jo paragraph maine delete kiya, jis mein main har cheez ke baare mein sahi tha, wo zero bhejta.

Yahi poora sauda ek jagah hai. Chalees minute ka kaam teen saal ke bharose aur cheh naye clients ke muqabil, aur ye sab us baat se tay hua ke pehle paanch minute mein maine kaun sa message bheja. Skill design ya code mein nahi thi. Skill jawab dene se pehle wale us pause mein thi.

## Revision request hamla nahi hai

Yahi cheez zyadatar freelancers galat samajhte hain, aur main bhi lambe arse tak galat samajhta raha.

Revision request aap ke talent par faisle jaisi mehsoos hoti hai. Wo nahi hai. Jab client wapas aa kar tabdeeli maangta hai, to wo aap par itna bharosa kar raha hai ke dobara poochta hai, bajaye iske ke khamoshi se chala jaaye aur kisi aur ko hire kar le. Ek seedha rejection jiske saath request lagi ho, wo baat ka khatma nahi hai. Wo baat mein bane rehne ki dawat hai.

Jo clients kabhi revision request nahi bhejte, wo khush wale nahi hote. Wo wo hote hain jo pehle se aap ke replacement se baat kar rahe hain.

## Calm ek skill hai, koi personality nahi

Log composure ko aisi cheez samajhte hain jo ya to aap ke paas hoti hai ya nahi. Aisa nahi hai. Ye ek aadat hai jo aap ek ek delete kiye gaye paragraph se banate hain.

Aap ka talent aap ko project dilata hai. Aap ka akhlaaq aap ko agla project dilata hai. Kaam darwaza kholta hai, lekin aap mushkil lamhe ko kaise handle karte hain, wo vague brief, wo chaar minute wala rejection, wo scope jo aap ki peeth peeche barh gaya, yahi tay karta hai ke darwaza khula rehta hai ya nahi. Calm ek skill hai. Composure deals close karti hai.

Iska matlab ye nahi ke aap scope ko hamesha bekaboo chalne dein ya kabhi push back na karein. Iska matlab hai ke aap dono kaamon ko alag kar dein. Pehle aap problem ko khatam karte hain. Phir, sukoon se aur sahi waqt par, aap brief, references, aur scope ki baat karte hain, defence ki jagah bharose ki position se.
