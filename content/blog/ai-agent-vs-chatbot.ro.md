"Chatbot" aur "AI agent" ko log aise use karte hain jaise dono ek hi cheez hon. Aise nahi hai. Aur is confusion ki qeemat businesses ko asal paison mein parti hai, kyunke log ek saadi chatbot khareed lete hain is umeed par ke woh poora operation chalayegi, ya phir aise kaam ke liye agent over-engineer kar dete hain jo ek chatbot se ho jaata. To chaliye isay saaf saaf tay kar lete hain.

## Chhota jawab

> Chatbot baat karti hai. AI agent kaam karta hai.

Chatbot ek conversation ke andar sawalon ke jawab deti hai. Aap poochte hain, woh jawab deti hai, aur aapke agle message ka intezaar karti hai. AI agent ko ek goal diya jaata hai aur phir woh us goal tak pohanchne ka poora multi-step kaam khud karta hai: steps khud sochta hai, tools use karta hai, apne result check karta hai, aur kuch ghalat ho to khud adjust kar leta hai.

Agar ek line yaad rakhni ho, to yahi rakhein. Neeche sab kuch isi ki tafseel hai.

## Chatbot asal mein karti kya hai

Chatbot conversation ke andar rehti hai. Iska kaam yeh samajhna hai ke aapne kya likha aur sahi maloomat ke sath jawab dena. Ek achi chatbot customer ke sawalon ke jawab de sakti hai, kisi ko aapki services samjha sakti hai, lead capture kar sakti hai, aur zaroorat parne par insaan ko hand off kar sakti hai.

Yeh sach mein faida mand hai, aur bohat se businesses ke liye bilkul itna hi kaafi hai. Aapki website par ek chatbot jo wohi chaalees sawal jo aapke customers hamesha poochte hain, din raat, har zubaan mein jawab de, yeh asli value hai.

Lekin chatbot jawab par ruk jaati hai. Woh jaa kar woh kaam *karti* nahi jo abhi bayan kiya. Usay kahein ke asal mein refund process karo, teen systems update karo, aur customer ko email bhejo, to saadi chatbot yeh nahi kar sakti. Woh sirf bata sakti hai ke kaise hoga.

## AI agent asal mein karta kya hai

AI agent woh kaam kar deta hai. Aap usay ek goal dete hain, ek sawal nahi, aur woh us goal ki taraf kai steps mein khud kaam karta hai, bina aapke har step par click kiye.

Ek agent aane wali email parh sakta hai, aapke database se zaroori record nikaal sakta hai, faisla kar sakta hai ke kya karna hai, apne tools ke zariye woh action le sakta hai, tracking sheet update kar sakta hai, aur sirf woh chand cases flag kar sakta hai jinhe sach mein insaan chahiye. Yeh poora silsila, jo khud decide aur execute hota hai, yahi usay ek "fancy autocomplete" se nikaal kar asal agent banata hai.

Sab se aasan test: agar woh sirf jawab deta hai, to chatbot hai. Agar woh sochta hai, tools use karta hai, aur kaam complete karta hai, to agent hai.

## Asal farq, nuqta ba nuqta

- **Goal vs sawal.** Chatbot ek waqt mein ek sawal sambhalti hai. Agent ek goal leta hai aur us tak poora raasta khud sambhalta hai.
- **Jawab vs amal.** Chatbot batati hai ke kya karna hai. Agent kar deta hai.
- **Bhool jaana vs yaad rakhna.** Chatbot aksar chat khatam hote hi bhool jaati hai. Agent context rakh sakta hai, memory use kar sakta hai, aur ek task ko kai steps aur systems mein le ja sakta hai.
- **Tools nahi vs tools.** Chatbot baat karti hai. Agent APIs, databases aur dusre software ko call kar ke asal kaam karwata hai.
- **Fixed vs adaptive.** Scripted chatbot tab toot-ti hai jab haqeeqat script se match na kare. Agent un-expected ke baare mein soch kar adjust kar sakta hai.

## Aapke business ko kaunsa chahiye?

Zyada tar businesses ko dono chahiye, alag alag jagah, aur sach yeh hai ke jawab kaam par depend karta hai, hype par nahi.

**Chatbot** chunein jab kaam maloomat dena aur rehnumai karna ho: customer ke sawalon ke jawab, leads qualify karna, services samjhana, basic support. Kaam conversation mein shuru aur khatam hota hai.

**AI agent** chunein jab kaam mukammal karna ho: requests ko end to end process karna, systems ke beech data move karna, multi-step kaam jaise onboarding ya order handling, jahan steps shakal mein ek jaise lekin tafseel mein har baar thora alag hon.

Ek kaam ki baat: agar aap kehne lagein "aur phir isay khud-ba-khud yeh karna chahiye...", to aap ek agent describe kar rahe hain. Agar aap "isay jawab dena chahiye..." par ruk jaate hain, to chatbot kaafi hai.

## Yeh line dhundli kyun ho rahi hai (aur kyun maayne rakhti hai)

Aaj kal ke assistants dono kaam karne lage hain. Isi site par maujood chat assistant aapke sawalon ke jawab chatbot ki tarah deta hai, lekin woh ek qualified lead capture kar ke hamari team tak bhi pohancha deta hai, jo agent wala kaam hai. Yahi rukh har cheez ka hai: upar se conversational, andar se asal kaam karne ke qaabil.

Yeh is liye maayne rakhta hai ke aap label nahi khareed rahe. Aap ek nateeja khareed rahe hain. Sahi sawal yeh kabhi nahi ke "mujhe chatbot chahiye ya agent?" Sahi sawal yeh hai ke "mujhe kaunsa kaam karwana hai, aur us mein se kitna mere baghair chalna chahiye?" Is ka jawab dein, sahi system khud zahir ho jaata hai.

## Khulasa
